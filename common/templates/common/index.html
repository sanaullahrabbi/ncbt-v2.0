ABOUT THE FOUNDER

The dream of a globally acclaimed institution promoted Mr. Nurul Haque Chowdhury to set up NCBT. Mr. Chowdhury visited many countries of the world and observed the way the universities of those countries work. He made up his mind to establish such an institution which will be the torch bearer of knowledge and wisdom. In spite of his busy schedule, he spent a lot of time to actualize his dream into reality. Being the chairman of Reliance industrial park, he has also associated himself with a variety of humanitarian works. In accordance with the slogan, 'Digital Bangladesh', Mr Haque wants to make the students go digital by setting up a college which excels in business and technology.

NCBT undertakes to build up a reputation as a rural level low cost digital education in Business and Technology with innovative research and development as a private institution.

Lab Facility

01

No science can survive without labs. NCBT has a spacious computer lab. Groomed with 50 computers the lab is an example of ultra-modern technology. The lab gives the most cutting-edge technical support to the students.

Hostel Facility

02

For the purpose of accommodating the students a modern residential complex is being built on the bank of the Modhumoti river. At least, 200 students will live in the yet to-be made complex. A makeshift hostel is currently at work.

Scholarship Facility

03

Students who have outstanding result at SSC and HSC levels are eligible for scholarships and financial assistance. Besides these, students who have excellent performance in the first and second semesters are given special scholarship.

Our Programs

N.Haque College of Business and Technology (NCBT) programs offered for students -

BBA

Bachelor of Business Administration

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) Program aims at training the boys and girls for this act and providing clear understanding of the functioning of business from theoretical, practical, and managerial perspectives.

CSE

Computer Science & Engineering

Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is an engineering discipline that covers several topics related to computation, programming languages, program design, computer hardware and software and integrates several fields of computer science.

HSC

Higher Secondary School Certificate

The Higher Secondary Certificate is a public examination credential in Bangladesh, India, and Pakistan. HSC is equivalent to GCE A Level in England and 3rd and 4th year of high schools in the United States.

ACADEMIC COLLABORATION

NCBT evaluates the role of academic networking around the world. An initiative has been taken to reach out to the famous universities of the world. Any students willing to go abroad for higher education can get the opportunity at NCBT. In the coming days, NCBT hopes to be linked to other universities of the world.

EXTRA - CURRICULAR ACTIVITIE

A sound mind in a sound body. Taking this truth into consideration, the NCBT authority puts emphasis on various indoor and outdoor games. Students can become members of the NCBT Football team or the NCBT cricket team. Except this, there is a good arrangement for playing Hokey, badminton and volleyball. NCBT patronizes art & culture.

The NCBT cultural club is an umbrella agency for all kinds of cultural activism. Drama, poetry and music are amongst the most attended genre of the cultural club. Students are also trained for debating inside and outside the campus. An active debating club directed by a senior lecturer is always striving for excellence.

The students who are not majoring in CSE are given the chance of doing a free course on computer. The NCBT English club is the place where students can practise speaking English. The club has also the power of arranging debating competitions.
