Admission Information

Admission Eligibility
From NCBT Admission Office.

Candidates seeking admission must apply in prescribed application Form which can be collected on payment of Tk. 1000.00 Candidates having 2nd division or at least GPA 2.50 in both SSC & HSC examinations, or their equivalents are eligible to apply for admission into Honours program in Law,English, Sociology and Business Administration. For B.Sc in (Computer Science & Engineering), B.Sc.( Electrical, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering) and B.Sc (Civil Engineering) programs, candidate must have physics & Mathematics at their HSC level.For Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm.).

Required Documents
From NCBT Admission Office.
For Undergraduate/ Honors Program:

- Original Transcript & Certificate of S.S.C. or Equivalent with 2 photocopies.

- Original Transcript & Certificate of H.S.C. or Equivalent with 2 photocopies and 7 Copies of Pass Port size Photograph .

For HSC Program:

- Original Transcript & Certificate of S.S.C. or Equivalent with 2 photocopies.

- 7 Copies of Pass Port size Photograph .

APPLICATION FOR ADMISSION

A candidate seeking admission to a program must procure prescribed Application From from DIU office on payment of TK. 1000.00. Admission form available at admission information section: On the basis of admission test result, a student will get full free scholarship.
