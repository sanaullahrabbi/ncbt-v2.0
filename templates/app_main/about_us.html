About US

Our History

N.Haque College of Business and Technology (NCBT) is one of the leading institutes affiliated with the National University of Bangladesh. N.Haque College of Business and Technology is a seat of higher education, situated in Gopalgonj, the district where the founder president of Bangladesh Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman was born. Our customized curriculum builds the knowledge, skill and attitudes that students need to succeed in their academic and professional careers after College & University. Students are encouraged to challenge themselves their partners and their teachers in a setting that gives them confidence and builds their skills to think independently.NCBT undertakes to build up a reputation as a rural level low cost digital education in Business and Technology with innovative research and development as a private institution.
