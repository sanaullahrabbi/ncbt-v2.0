About Us

Welcome To NCBT

N.Haque College of Business and Technology (NCBT) is one of the leading institutes affiliated with the National University of Bangladesh. N.Haque College of Business and Technology is a seat of higher education, situated in Gopalgonj, the district where the founder president of Bangladesh Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman was born. Our customized curriculum builds the knowledge, skill and attitudes that students need to succeed in their academic and professional careers after College & University. Students are encouraged to challenge themselves their partners and their teachers in a setting that gives them confidence and builds their skills to think independently.NCBT undertakes to build up a reputation as a rural level low cost digital education in Business and Technology with innovative research and development as a private institution.

ABOUT THE FOUNDER

The dream of a globally acclaimed institution promoted Mr. Nurul Haque Chowdhury to set up NCBT. Mr. Chowdhury visited many countries of the world and observed the way the universities of those countries work. He made up his mind to establish such an institution which will be the torch bearer of knowledge and wisdom. In spite of his busy schedule, he spent a lot of time to actualize his dream into reality. Being the chairman of Reliance industrial park, he has also associated himself with a variety of humanitarian works. In accordance with the slogan, 'Digital Bangladesh', Mr Haque wants to make the students go digital by setting up a college which excels in business and technology.

NCBT undertakes to build up a reputation as a rural level low cost digital education in Business and Technology with innovative research and development as a private institution.

Our Mission

The mission of NCBT is to contribute, through collaborative research and education to efforts to create a Digital Bangladesh. NCBT pays due attention to the spread of technological knowledge everywhere.

Our Vision

NCBT aspires to be a world class teaching and capacity development institution that addresses the needs of present and future generation. It endeavors to provide global leadership on the aspects of business and technology. NCBT also seeks to become a widely recognized international graduate teaching and research institution known for-

 - Strict adherence to rigorous scientific method.
 - Emphasis on issues providing high quality services & outputs.
 - A focus on issues that impact sustainability.
 - High ethical standard along with academic excellence.
 - Intellectual vitality and spirit of cooperation.
 - Equality and openness in access to opportunities.
 - Strict adherence to the principle of gender balance.
 - Going carbon free amongst all campuses in Bangladesh.
